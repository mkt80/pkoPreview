Complete the following steps to sign up for a great year of paddling with Pale Kai Outrigger!

#1: Create (or Update) your SCORA Account

New Paddlers: Create your SCORA account at www.scoraregistration.com/paddler_register. Make a note of your SCORA ID. Once registration is complete, you will be sent to SmartWaiver to sign your waiver.

Returning Paddlers: Log into your SCORA account at www.scoraregistration.com/paddler_login. Make a note of your SCORA ID. You will be sent to SmartWaiver to sign your waiver. Once that's complete, you'll be sent back to the registration site to update your account information.

You will be emailed a link to your completed SmartWaiver. Download and SAVE IT! You'll need it for the next step.

#2: PKO Member Registration on TeamSnap
