Complete the following steps to sign up for a great year of paddling with Pale Kai Outrigger!

#1: Create (or Update) your SCORA Account

Go to https://www.scora.org/login/. New paddlers will need to create a SCORA account. Make a note of your SCORA ID. Once your SCORA registration is complete, you will be sent to SmartWaiver to sign your waiver.

Returning paddlers will be sent back to the SCORA registration page to update their account info.

You will be emailed a link to your completed SmartWaiver. Download and SAVE IT! You'll need it for the next step.

#2: PKO Member Registration on TeamSnap

Note that PKO member registration doesn't open until March 1st. Sign up for the newsletter below to be informed when regisration opens up.
