Complete the following steps to sign up for a great year of paddling with Pale Kai Outrigger!

#1: Create (or Update) your SCORA Account

Go to https://www.scora.org/login/. New paddlers will need to create a SCORA account. Make a note of your SCORA ID. Once your SCORA registration is complete, you will be sent to Smartwaiver to sign your waiver.

Returning paddlers need only sign the Smartwaiver.

You will be emailed a link to your completed Smartwaiver. Download and SAVE IT! You'll need it for the next step. (Be aware that the link is only good for three days, so don't hesitate.)

#2: Annual PKO Member Registration

Note that annual PKO member registration officially open until March 1st, though we usually do a soft open around February 1st. Sign up for the newsletter, below, to be informed when regisration opens up.

PKO uses TeamSnap to manage our membership roster, collect dues, schedule workouts, races, and other events, and to communicate with members. When you tap the button below, you'll be taken to TeamSnap where you can register for the current year.
