Pale Kai Outrigger is a non-profit outrigger canoe club located in Avila Beach, California near the city of San Luis Obispo, and is a member of the Southern California Outrigger Racing Association (SCORA). We offer competitive Hawaiian style outrigger canoe racing for men and women right here on the beautiful central coast waters of California.
